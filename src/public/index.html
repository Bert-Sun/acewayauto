Why Us?

High-Quality OE-Spec Auto Parts

ACEWAY Canada Inc. is a Toronto-based Canadian company that is committed to warehouse distribution of FREY-branded high-quality OE-spec aftermarket auto parts for European makes. Especially for Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi and Volkswagen, across Canada.

Sole Agent in Canada

As the sole agent of the brand FREY, ACEWAY will make a great contribution to the existing auto parts market in Canada where more cost-competitive high-quality products with more comprehensive categories for popular European models are badly needed, and here comes the brand FREY!
